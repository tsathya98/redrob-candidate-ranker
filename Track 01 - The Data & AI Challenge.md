# Data & AI Challenge: Intelligent Candidate Discovery

## Track 01: The Data & AI Challenge

**Challenge Theme:** Intelligent Candidate Discovery  
**Hosted by:** RedRob AI × Hack2Skill  
**Target Participants:** AI/ML Engineers, Data Scientists, Developers, Search & Retrieval Specialists, and LLM Practitioners

---

## Overview

Are you ready to redefine the future of talent acquisition?

Recruiters are tasked with finding the perfect fit from oceans of profiles, but traditional keyword filters are simply not cutting it. They miss the hidden gems — the candidates whose true potential, intent, and subtle behavioral signals are lost in the noise.

There has to be a smarter way.

This challenge invites you to build an AI brain for modern hiring: an intelligent system that can move beyond surface-level keywords, deeply understand context, predict relevance, and transform massive talent pools into precise, actionable shortlists.

Join us to build the next generation of predictive ranking engines.

---

## Your Mission: The Core Objective

Develop a robust, workable Proof of Concept that does not just filter candidates, but intelligently ranks them.

Your system should act as the ultimate AI recruiter, capable of understanding job requirements, candidate context, and behavioral signals to produce a highly relevant ranked shortlist.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Deep Job Understanding

Interpret complex and nuanced job descriptions.

The system should understand not just the keywords in a job description, but also the intent, role expectations, required experience level, technical depth, domain context, and hidden hiring priorities.

---

### 2. Contextual Relevance

See beyond keywords to understand semantic fit.

The system should be able to identify candidates who are meaningfully aligned with the job, even when their profiles do not exactly match the wording of the job description.

---

### 3. Signal Integration

Leverage all available data, including:

- Profile attributes
- Skills and experience
- Career metadata
- Activity signals
- Behavioral signals
- Engagement indicators
- Any other relevant structured or unstructured candidate data

The goal is to combine multiple signals into a reliable ranking mechanism.

---

### 4. Ranked Candidate Output

Deliver a lightning-fast, highly accurate, and expertly ranked shortlist of the best-fit candidates.

The final system should generate a ranked output file in the predefined format, clearly detailing the recommended candidate shortlist.

---

## Innovation Freedom

There is no fixed architecture.

Participants are encouraged to unleash their innovation and bring their best creativity to the solution. You may use any suitable approach, including but not limited to:

- Rule-based scoring
- Embedding-based semantic search
- LLM-based reasoning
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation
- Hybrid ranking models
- Learning-to-rank methods
- Feature engineering
- Behavioral signal scoring
- Explainable AI ranking

The focus is on building a practical, intelligent, and effective candidate discovery system.

---

## Dataset

Participants will be provided with a structured challenge dataset containing candidate-related information and relevant signals.

Your task is to use this dataset to build a working candidate ranking Proof of Concept.

---

## Submission Checklist

Show us your brilliance. Your submission must include the following:

### 1. The Code

A well-organized GitHub repository containing your complete implementation.

The repository should include all necessary source code, configuration files, scripts, and dependencies required to run the solution.

---

### 2. The Blueprint

Clear documentation or a README file explaining:

- Your methodology
- Technical choices
- System architecture
- Ranking logic
- Data processing steps
- Model or scoring strategy
- Assumptions made
- How to run the solution

---

### 3. The Results

A ranked output file in the predefined format.

This file should detail your recommended candidate shortlist and clearly represent the final ranking produced by your system.

---

## Expected Outcome

By the end of the challenge, your solution should demonstrate how AI can help recruiters move beyond traditional keyword search and identify the most relevant candidates using deeper contextual and behavioral understanding.

The final Proof of Concept should be:

- Practical
- Explainable
- Scalable
- Fast
- Accurate
- Relevant to real-world hiring workflows

---

## Final Goal

Build an intelligent candidate discovery and ranking engine that can transform large talent pools into precise, actionable shortlists for modern hiring.
